A passion to be shared with a world :)

Welcome to home of handmade candles. All my work is made from scratch, from the vessel to the wax. Candles are made using soy wax, cotton or wooden knots, scented oils and either concrete or clay vessels. Making candles is a passion I try to share with others. So take a seat, light a candle and enjoy beautiful scents in your room.
